Darío Gómez

Known as El rey del despecho

During his lifetime, Darío:

  • Began writing verses at age 14 and seeking support while working as a mechanic and farmer
  • He decided to integrate with his brother Heriberto Gómez in the group Los Legendarios
  • His first great success is Ángel perdido, inspired by the death of his sister Rosangela, on 31 October 1978
  • In 1985, Darío Gómez made his debut as a soloist and scored a new triumph when he performed Decidelo (Decide it);
  • He released his first full length with the name of Así se le canta al despecho (So it is sung to spite)
  • On 14 May 2015, Gómez released the video of "Me voy a casar (I'm going to marry)" next to the King of Popular Music Jhonny Rivera
